Industry Classification Systems:
- NAICS (North American Industry Classification System): Classifies industries by a hierarchical coding system.
- SOC (Standard Occupational Classification): Classifies workers into job-related categories.
- GICS (Global Industry Classification Standard): Classifies companies into sectors, industry groups, industries, and sub-industries.
- SIC (Standard Industrial Classification): Older system for classifying industries by a four-digit code.
